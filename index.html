Sanchar Saathi Portal

A citizen-centric initiative to empower mobile subscribers, enhance their security, and promote awareness of government initiatives.

About Sanchar Saathi

The Sanchar Saathi portal is a government initiative to help mobile subscribers protect themselves against fraud, report stolen devices, and stay aware of important communication security measures.

Services Offered

  • CHAKSHU: Report suspected fraud and suspicious communications.
  • CEIR: Block your lost or stolen mobile device through the Central Equipment Identity Register.
  • TAFCOP: Know your mobile connections and check for unauthorized usage.
  • KYM: Verify your mobile's IMEI number and ensure it's not counterfeit.
  • RICWIN: Report international calls received with an Indian number.
  • KYI: Find out your wireless internet provider (ISP).
